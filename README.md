# pmp-certification-journey

**A working log of the Project Management Professional certification process — study notes, resources, timelines, and lessons learned.**

---

## Why This Exists

The PMP certification is one of the most recognized credentials in project management. Getting there requires structured study, real project experience, and passing one of the harder professional exams in the field. This repository documents that journey in real time — not just the result, but the process.

If you're considering the PMP, this is the resource I wish I'd had.

---

## About Me

I'm approaching the PMP from an unconventional background — former teacher, military spouse, and now founder of NearBase, an AI-powered preschool finder for military families. Project management isn't new to me — running a classroom is project management, and so is relocating a family across the country six times — but formalizing that experience into a credential is the goal.
